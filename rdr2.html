Red Dead Redemption 2

Red Dead Redemption 2 is an open-world action adventure game developed by Rockstar Games. The story follows Arthur Morgan, an outlaw living in the American Wild West. Players explore a massive world, complete missions, hunt animals and experience a deep story about loyalty and survival.

Genre: Open World Action
Developer: Rockstar Games
Release: June 26 2018
