The Witcher 3

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is a story-driven RPG developed by CD Projekt Red. Players control Geralt of Rivia, a monster hunter searching for his adopted daughter in a dangerous fantasy world. The game features a large open world, meaningful choices and many quests that affect the story.

Genre: Open World Action
Developer: CD PROJEKT RED
Release: May 18 2015
