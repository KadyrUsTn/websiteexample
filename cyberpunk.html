Cyberpunk

Cyberpunk 2077 is an open-world role playing game developed by CD Projekt Red. The game takes place in Night City, a futuristic city full of advanced technology, crime and powerful corporations. Players take the role of a mercenary named V and explore a world filled with missions, cybernetic upgrades and dangerous enemies.

Genre: Open World Action
Developer: CD PROJEKT RED
Release: December 10 2020
